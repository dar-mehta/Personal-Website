About Me

I am Dar Mehta, an 18 year old Mechatronics Engineering student in my 1A term at the University of Waterloo. My passion lies in taking on challenging and innovative projects to keep pace with the dynamic and exciting world of technology.

My first exposure to robotics was with my high school's VEX Robotics team from which I branched out to the FIRST Robotics Competition. The combination of my passion for hardware and software development led to my ever-growing love for robotics and embedded systems.

My hobbies include watching and playing sports, particularly hockey. I also love gaming, hanging out with friends and family, watching movies, travelling and learning new things!
